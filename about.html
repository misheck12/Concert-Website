Kalibu 2022 youth Summit

The Kalibu Conference is the first conference to host a high school graduate career path. This exciting event will include pitching, learning, and mentorship opportunities for attendees. This is a great opportunity for high school graduates to explore different career options and get expert advice from professionals in the field. So mark your calendars and plan to attend the Kalibu Conference!
